    PLACEMENT

    The enterprising Training and Placement Cell at Karpagam Academy of Higher Education (KAHE) gears the students to be resourceful assets to their future employing organizations. Towards this, students are prepared with need-based training right from the first year onwards. Expert trainers from the industry impart their experience to enlighten the students. Therefore, comprehensive training in aptitude, soft skills as well as technical expertise is extended to bring about a well-rounded development in the students. Interviews and campus visits are much looked forward to, and every year, an increasing number of companies are making their presence felt at campus recruitment. The current year has seen more than 93% (300 students) get placed in over 20 companies; the expected placement for the next academic year is eyed to be an impressive figure of 400 students.
